Welcome to the Emergency Communication Center (ECC), Uyo!

The team is always on ground day and night to receive and despatch all emergency calls for the purpose of saving lives. It's like a soldiers job, so, we're battle-ready at all times!

Mission

Handling all calls appropriately with utmost attention, tact, and professionalism, ensuring that emergency/distress calls are despatched to enable the various response agencies quickly deal with the situation, while educating hoax callers and other members of the public on the purpose of the 112 emergency helpline in order to reduce, if not completely stop, its abuse.
